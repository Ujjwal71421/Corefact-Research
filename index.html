About Us

Founded in 2023 and headquartered in Delhi, Corefact Research was born from a shared passion for uncovering the true drivers behind market behavior. Our seasoned team of researchers, data scientists, and industry strategists collaborates closely with brands, institutions, and startups to design and execute end-to-end market research engagements. From survey programming and expert interviews to advanced analytics and predictive modeling, we tailor every project to your unique objectives and audience.

Our proprietary online panels, combined with cutting-edge technology platforms, ensure rapid data collection and rigorous quality control. Whether you’re gauging consumer sentiment around a new product, mapping competitive landscapes, or assessing brand health, our approach delivers both depth and agility. At Corefact Research, we believe that every insight—quantitative or qualitative—must translate into strategic advantage. By transforming complex datasets into clear, actionable recommendations, we empower our clients to navigate dynamic markets with confidence and seize opportunities ahead of the curve.
